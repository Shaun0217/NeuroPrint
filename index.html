Neuroscience Education Project

Hold the brain.
Understand it.

NeuroPrint combines 3D-printed anatomical models with scannable region nodes, making the brain tangible, visual, and accessible for every student.

How it works

Physical model. Digital depth.

Each brain region on the physical model has a scannable QR code that opens a dedicated learning page, bridging the tactile and the digital.

3D Printed Brain Models

Anatomically accurate, hand-held models showing all major cortical regions. Designed for classroom exploration, patient education, and independent study.

Scannable Region Nodes

QR codes embedded on each brain region link directly to detailed explanations, interactive diagrams, and targeted exercises. No app download required.

Classroom Curriculum

Lesson plans, printable worksheets, and assessment guides built for K–12 educators. Every resource is aligned with neuroscience learning standards.
