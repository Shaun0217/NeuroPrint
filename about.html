About the Project

Our Mission

This project integrates neuroscience and technology to develop a 3D-printed, interactive brain model designed to enhance understanding of the human brain's structure and functions. Each major brain region will feature a scannable QR code linked to a custom-built website, providing users with detailed descriptions, visual aids, and additional resources about the specific region's role in cognition, behavior, and physiology. By combining hands-on interaction with digital learning, this innovative approach aims to make complex neurological concepts accessible and engaging for students, educators, and enthusiasts alike. The project also highlights the synergy between modern technology and education, emphasizing the importance of integrating STEM disciplines to foster curiosity and deeper knowledge.
