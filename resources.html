Curriculum Aligned

Designed to work alongside the physical model.

Each resource in this kit was developed to complement, not replace, hands-on exploration. Students engage first with the physical 3D model, then deepen their understanding through structured written activities.

All materials are standards-referenced for K-12 life science and biology. They require no special software, are printable from any device, and are editable so you can adapt them to your class.
